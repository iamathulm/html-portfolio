Top 3 Favorite Tamil movies of all time!

Master-Lokesh Kanagaraj

An ex-alcoholic turned professer, JD (Thalapthy Vijay) sets out on a journey of self discovery when a drug lord Bhavani (Vijay Sethupathi) begins kidnapping and killing innocent for his nefarious smuggling purposes.

Mersal- Atlee

Vetri and Maran (Thalapathy Vijay) set out to avenge the death of their father at the hands of a doctor who aims to turn medicine into the world's biggest business by privatizing services and charging excess fees from needy patients.

Varisu- Vamshi Paidipally

Vijay, a successful entrepreuner returns to his family to take over his family business on behest of his ailing father. In time he discovers the deep rifts between his family as each one is trying to get rich and vying for the business through unfair means.
